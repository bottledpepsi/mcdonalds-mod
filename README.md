# 🍟 McDonald's Mod — *I'm Lovin' It*

> Craft, cook, and eat your way through a full McDonald's menu — with real multi-step recipes, custom effects, and a brand new ore tier.

---

## 🍔 What Is This Mod?

McDonald's Mod brings the iconic fast food experience into Minecraft with **genuine crafting depth**. This isn't just a food item dump, every menu item has a **multi-step recipe chain** that makes you actually work for your meal. Build tools, prep ingredients, cook them, then assemble the final product. It feels earned.

On top of the food, the mod introduces **Mcium**, a rare new ore that spawns deep underground and is harder to mine than Netherite. Smelt it into ingots and craft a **full Mcium tool set** with custom attack damage, and enchantability.

## 🍳 Recipe System

Food doesn't just fall out of a crafting table. You'll need to **prep ingredients** before cooking:

**Fries chain:**
1. Craft a **Potato Cutter**
2. Use cutter on a potato → **Raw Fries**
3. Smelt Raw Fries → **Fried Potatoes**
4. Combine Fried Potatoes + Paper for sized portions:
   - `[F][Paper]` → Small Fries
   - `[F][F][Paper]` → Medium Fries
   - `[FF][FP]` → Large Fries
   - `[FF]` → Hashbrown

**Nuggets chain:**
1. Surround raw chicken with wheat → **8x Raw Nuggets**
2. Smelt each Raw Nugget → **McNugget**

**Filet-O-Fish chain:**
1. Wheat above + below cod or salmon → **Raw Filet**
2. Smelt → **Filet-O-Fish**

**Apple Pie chain:**
1. Wheat + Apple + Sugar in crafting → **Raw Apple Pie**
2. Smelt → **Apple Pie**

**Simple recipes:**
- Big Mac: `[Bread / Cooked Beef / Bread]`
- McRib: `[Bread / Cooked Porkchop / Bread]`
- McFlurry: Milk Bucket + Sugar + Packed Ice
- Cokes: Paper + Sugar

## ⛏️ Mcium

Mcium is a **rare, fully buried ore** that generates deep underground (y=-64 to y=16), never exposed to cave air. You'll need to strip mine to find it.

**Stats vs. Netherite:**
| Stat | Netherite | Mcium |
|------|-----------|-------|
| Block hardness | 50 | **55** |
| Blast resistance | 1200 | **1200** |
| Pickaxe durability | 2031 | **1650** |
| Sword durability | 2031 | **1470** |
| Axe durability | 2031 | **1560** |
| Pickaxe damage | +1.0 | **+1.0** |
| Sword damage | +8 | **+9** |

**Full tool set:** Pickaxe · Sword · Axe · Shovel · Hoe

All tools support the **full enchantment table** — Efficiency, Fortune, Silk Touch, Unbreaking, Sharpness (sword/axe), Fire Aspect, Looting, and Curse of Vanishing.
